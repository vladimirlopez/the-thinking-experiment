# The Updated LinkedIn Blog Visibility Strategy

This revised strategy is based on LinkedIn's March 2024 update, which removed the Creator Mode toggle and integrated its features directly into the standard profile.

---

### 1. Optimize Your Profile (The "New" Creator Mode)

This is the most critical first step. By making "Follow" your primary button, you are essentially opting into the modern creator-style profile.

*   **Action:** Go to `Settings & Privacy` -> `Visibility` -> `Followers` and toggle **"Make Follow primary"** to **Yes**. This is the new "on switch" for being a creator on LinkedIn.
*   **Profile Headline & Banner:** Use your headline to state your value (e.g., "Physics Educator & AI in Teaching Advocate | Exploring pedagogy and technology at The Thinking Experiment blog.") and create a custom banner for your blog.
*   **Custom Button:** Add a custom button to your profile intro to link *directly* to your blog for a clear call-to-action.

### 2. The "Value-First" Posting Method

This part of the strategy is more important than ever. The quality of your content is what will make you stand out.

*   **Write a Mini-Article:** For each blog post, write a native LinkedIn post of 3-5 short paragraphs. Start with a strong hook, summarize the key takeaway, and end with a question to your audience.
*   **Link in the First Comment:** This tactic remains the gold standard for maximizing reach. State in your post, "Link to the full article in the comments below."
*   **Strategic Hashtags:** Use 5-7 relevant hashtags *in each post* for discovery (e.g., `#AIinEducation #EdTech #PhysicsTeaching #ScienceCommunication #STEM`).
*   **Visuals:** Always include an image or a visual with your posts.

### 3. Leverage the Newsletter

You no longer need to hunt for a setting to enable this. It's the most direct way to notify your most engaged followers about new content.

*   **Action:** Go to the "Write article" section from your homepage and select the option to **"Create a newsletter."**
*   **Name:** Name it "The Thinking Experiment" to align with your blog's branding.

### 4. Engage Authentically

The principles of community management are timeless and crucial for growth.

*   **Comment Thoughtfully:** Spend 15 minutes a day finding posts under relevant hashtags and leave insightful comments. This makes you visible to a wider, targeted audience.
*   **Respond to Everyone:** When people comment on your posts, reply to them. This builds community and signals to the algorithm that your content is valuable, further boosting its reach.
